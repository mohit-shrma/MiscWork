Download the app

To download BandBaaja for Android you can scan the barcode to the left or follow following steps
  1. Open the Android Market application on your mobile phone.
  2. In the Android Market application, click the search button and write 'bandbaaja'
  3. Click on the BandBaaja application when it appears in the search results.
  4. Click the install button at the bottom of the page and click Ok on the next screen.
  5. The application is now being downloaded to the phone and you will get a notification when it is ready for use.
